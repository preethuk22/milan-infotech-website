Our Journey

The Milan Infotech Story

From a Bangalore startup to a multi-city enterprise IT powerhouse — our journey has been defined by consistent growth, strong partnerships, and client trust.

2007
Founded in Bangalore
Milan Infotech Pvt Ltd was incorporated in Bangalore, Karnataka, with a clear focus on IT staffing and software services for the BFSI and manufacturing sectors.
2010
Expanding into Engineering Services
Entered the automotive and aerospace engineering services domain, partnering with tier-1 players like Tata Motors, Airbus, and SAFRAN.
2013
ERP Practice Launched
Launched a dedicated SAP and Oracle ERP practice, becoming a trusted implementation and support partner for large enterprises across India.
2016
Global Partnership with BCC Services, France
Signed a strategic partnership with BCC Services of France, extending our delivery capabilities to European clients and enabling global ERP excellence.
2019
Multi-City Expansion
Opened captive centers in Pune and Hyderabad — strengthening our pan-India presence and talent pool.
2022+
200+ Professionals, 50+ Clients
Today, Milan Infotech employs over 200 IT professionals, serving 50+ enterprise clients across 10+ industries with end-to-end IT solutions.
