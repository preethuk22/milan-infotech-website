Zoypg.com
Trusted PG Discovering & Management Platform
PropTech · Rental Housing

Find and Manage Your Perfect PG

Zoypg.com is a comprehensive platform that brings together PG seekers and PG owners under one trusted digital roof. Whether you are a working professional looking for a safe, verified paying guest accommodation or a property owner wanting to efficiently manage your listings and tenants, Zoypg delivers a seamless end-to-end experience.

With verified listings, transparent pricing, and smart management tools, Zoypg removes the friction from PG discovery and rental management across India's cities.

🔍
Verified PG Listings
All accommodations are verified before listing — photos, amenities, pricing, and location details are reviewed to ensure accuracy and trust.
📍
Location-Based Discovery
Search PGs by locality, proximity to office or college, budget, and preferences like gender, meal type, and furnishing.
🧑‍💼
Owner Management Dashboard
PG owners can manage vacancies, track rent payments, handle tenant onboarding, and communicate with residents from a single dashboard.
💰
Transparent Pricing
No hidden charges. Seekers see the full cost — rent, deposit, and included amenities — upfront before connecting with any owner.
⭐
Ratings & Reviews
Genuine tenant reviews help future seekers make informed decisions, and help owners build a trusted reputation over time.
📲
Mobile-First Experience
Designed for on-the-go use — browse, shortlist, and connect with PG owners directly through a clean, intuitive mobile interface.

The Smart Way to Hire and Drive

Gadiwala is a mobility platform that connects professional drivers with vehicle owners who need reliable, on-demand driving services. Built for trust, safety, and flexibility — Gadiwala empowers drivers to build a career on their own schedule while giving owners peace of mind with verified, insured, and rated drivers.

Whether you need a driver for a single trip or want to schedule a monthly arrangement, Gadiwala brings structure, safety, and convenience to personal vehicle management.

Gadiwala
Book My Driver — Professional Driver Booking Platform
Mobility · On-Demand Drivers

  • Create a verified profile with Driving Licence, photo, and work experience

ExChek
Fintech Platform for Cross-Border Payments and Trade Finance
Fintech · Trade Finance · Forex

Global Trade. Simplified.

ExChek Fintech Private Limited is a specialized financial technology platform built for businesses that operate across borders. ExChek addresses the most complex pain points in international trade — from cross-border payment execution to regulatory compliance under RBI and FEMA guidelines — through a single, secure, and intuitive platform.

Designed for importers, exporters, and treasury teams, ExChek combines deep fintech expertise with robust technology infrastructure to make global trade operations faster, more transparent, and fully compliant.

🌐
Cross-Border Payment Platform
A secure, live platform for faster international payments — reducing settlement time and eliminating unnecessary intermediary friction.
📋
Trade Finance Operations
Specialized tools for managing Trade Finance, Letters of Credit (LCs), Incoterms, and SWIFT messaging in one unified workflow.
⚖️
Regulatory Compliance
Built-in features to ensure compliance with RBI and FEMA regulations in India — reducing risk for every cross-border transaction.
💹
Forex Management
Comprehensive foreign exchange and currency tracking tools to manage forex exposure and optimize conversion rates for global trade.
